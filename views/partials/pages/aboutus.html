About Us - CREBP SRA


The CREBP SRA is a suite of tools to speed up steps in the Systematic Review (SR) process. We are based at Centre for Research in Evidence-Based Practice, Bond University.
The SRA is freely available for anyone in the world to use. Simply register here with your name and email address. The SRA is a modular design which means the individual tools can be incorporated into existing SR workflows and combined with other automation tools.


Our tools are being continually reviewed and refined with additional tools being developed. Contant Us or email a team member below to suggest improvements or report problems.
